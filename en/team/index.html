Carolina Carreiro

Graduated in Law from the University of Lisbon (2009) and holding a postgraduate degree in Forensic Legal Sciences from the University of the Azores (2023/2024), Carolina Carreiro joined the Portuguese Bar Association in 2009 and began her individual legal practice in 2012, working across various areas of law.

From 2013 to 2021, she served as a lawyer at Novo Banco dos Açores, S.A., where she also held the position of Secretary-General. Since 2021, she has been a partner at Ricardo Pacheco & Associados, focusing on Civil Law and Notarial Services.

She has delivered legal training at several institutions and participated in the 2nd Banking Law Congress. Carolina has been a Member of the Fiscal Council of the Ilhas em Movimento Association since 2008 and President of the Jurisdictional Council of the Physical Activities Club of the Ponta Delgada Volunteer Firefighters since 2018.

Areas of Practice:

Civil Law | Notary Services

Verónica Casimiro

Graduated in Law from Universidade Lusófona do Porto and registered with the Portuguese Bar Association. She also holds a degree in History from the University of the Azores and a master's degree in Environment, Health, and Occupational Safety.

She works in the areas of Family Law, Criminal Law, Labor Law, and Civil Liability, focusing on practical and efficient resolution of legal matters, both judicially and extrajudicially.

She regularly collaborates with media outlets including Açoriano Oriental, Açores 9, Rádio Atlântida, and TSF, providing legal analysis on current topics.

Areas of Practice:

Workplace Accidents | Traffic Accidents | Family and Juvenile Law | Criminal Law

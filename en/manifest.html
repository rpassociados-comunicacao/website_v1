Manifest

About Us at RP & Associados

RP & Associados is a law firm headquartered in Ponta Delgada, in the Azores. Founded in 2010 by Ricardo Pacheco, the firm was established with a commitment to practicing law with ethics, precision, and a close relationship with clients. Later, Carolina Carreiro joined the project, contributing to a team that brings together complementary backgrounds and a shared vision of legal practice. Since then, we have developed our work in a sustainable way, guided by the principles of professional responsibility and respect for the core values of the legal profession.

A Nossa Visão da Advocacia

Acreditamos que a advocacia deve estar ao serviço da justiça, da dignidade humana e da confiança mútua. Atuamos com uma abordagem ética e humanizada na prestação de serviços jurídicos, valorizando o rigor na análise e o acompanhamento individualizado. A nossa missão é exercer a advocacia com responsabilidade e empenho, promovendo o esclarecimento e a defesa de direitos, com respeito pelos princípios fundamentais do Direito.

Integrity

Each case is handled with meticulous attention to detail and full professional responsibility. We understand that behind every legal matter lies a unique context and decisions with real impact.

Trust

An essential value in the practice of law. We strive to provide transparent and clear legal support, grounded in active listening, clear communication, and professional loyalty.

Professionalism

We operate with technical precision and full respect for ethical standards, fully aware of our role as legal professionals and of the trust our clients place in us.

Commitment

Our team stays up to date and focused on building legal solutions tailored to each individual case, promoting clarity at every stage of the process.

Availability

Being present means providing timely support, clarifying questions, and responding effectively to the needs brought to us. We maintain a close, human, and responsible relationship with those who seek our help.

At RP & Associados, we put the law at the service of people. We work with integrity, trust, and dedication, guided by the principles of legality, professional ethics, and justice.

Our partnerships

RP & Associados maintains a partnership with the American firm Barton Gilman, with presence in the United States, Bermuda, and Canada.

This collaboration enables the provision of legal support to Portuguese clients or those of Portuguese descent who reside in these countries and require legal assistance in Portugal.

Representation in inheritance and estate distribution proceedings

Acquisition, sale, or regularization of real estate in Portugal

Civil registry and Portuguese nationality matters

Recognition and enforcement of foreign judicial decisions

Legal support in family proceedings, such as divorces or regulation of parental responsibilities
